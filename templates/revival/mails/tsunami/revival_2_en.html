Proposing the right offer to a customer considerably increases the chances of buying. It’s by this way that multinationals keep their earnings evolving. They designed customized offers that suits every customer profile : public officer, Student, Bend-skinner or Bayam-Sellam.

How do they get to know their customers profiles ? To send them the right offer ?

Everything starts with the ability to follow your customers individually. You have to know who they are, their gender, their interests, their purchasing power, etc. That lets you able to propose them exactly what they were expected. While you can not be next to your customers 24/24, everything remains possible.


These are three simple steps to deeply know your customers.

A smart customer directory
Invite your customers to suscribe to your CCM (Cloud Customer Manager), then group them by profiles. It’s simple, you can create differents profiles and then add each registered customer to his matching one. A customer can even have multiple profiles. E.g : a person can be part of Man and Student profiles at the same time. Thus, you will better know who your customers are, and afterwards, who their friends are.

An online offer catalog
An online catalog enables you to propose your offers to hundreds, or even thousands of people at the same time. This because they do not need to contact you personnally and sometimes have to face your unavailability. You don’t have to repeat yourself over and over and get exhausted because of that. And if you are organized enough, your customers can directly get and pay your offer online. Like multinationals, customers can remotely pay for your service, without manual intervention.

Targeted revival
Now that you master your customers interests and have your catalog online, stay close to them by constant Mails and SMS revivals. Each customer will receive the offer tailored to his profile. Two types of revivals exist.

  • Smart revivals :
    • Once you have set your customers profiles, every offer/product of your catalog is directly suggested to potentially interest people when you publish it.

  • Cyclical revivals :
    • You are the one configuring it, and they run in a totally autonomous way. Thus for a given profile, you can decide to send a precise message, on specific dates of month, some precise days of the week, or on a fixed interval of days. You can also, set the exact time on which the message will be sent, and decide the way: Mail, SMS, or the both. Example : If you own a restaurant and cook a vegetarian food on weekends, you can plan the reminder on Fridays and Saturdays at 11 to all your vegetarian clients. And thus, they will not forget to visit you, or directly order online.
